Koï Manufacturing Execution System

Koï M.E.S. is a very specialised piece of software helping you to follow what happens on the production floor. It only does that but does it well. It is meant to be highly productive.

Koï is made for small and mid-size structures, 5 to 20 people on the production floor working on discrete stuff. It is efficient especially in agile environments (i.e. many different jobs instead of a few recurring ones).

Koï is made with reliability in mind. It's been in production use since 2013.
